What is EVG Fine Check?

Emirates Vehicle Gate (EVG) is the UAE's official platform that consolidates traffic fines from all seven emirates in one place.

Use the official EVG Fine Check portal to instantly view all outstanding fines online.

EVG connects all UAE traffic authorities into a single system for real-time updates.
