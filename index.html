Traffic Fines

What is EVG Fine Check?

Emirates Vehicle Gate (EVG) is the UAE's official platform that consolidates traffic fines from all seven emirates in one place. Whether you have a speeding violation on Sheikh Zayed Road, a parking fine in Abu Dhabi, or a Salik toll issue in Dubai — everything is accessible through a single portal.

The evg fine check service allows UAE vehicle owners to instantly view outstanding fines using their plate number, Emirates ID, or Traffic Code Number (TCN) from their Mulkiya — no registration required for basic lookups.

⚠️ Unpaid EVG fines attract a 50% late penalty automatically. No warning is sent — the charge is added as soon as the grace period passes.

EVG connects with RTA Dubai, Abu Dhabi Police, Sharjah Police and other traffic authorities across the UAE, giving you a unified view of all violations across emirates in real time.

Step by Step

How to Check EVG Fines Online

Checking your EVG traffic fines takes less than two minutes. Follow these steps or refer to the full guide on https://evgfinescheck.com:

  1. Visit evg.ae or open the EVG mobile app on your phone.
  2. Log in with your UAE Pass or Emirates ID credentials.
  3. Enter your plate number, Traffic Code Number (TCN), or Emirates ID.
  4. View all active fines — amounts, violation locations, black points, and photos.
  5. Pay securely using credit/debit card, Apple Pay, or bank transfer.
  6. Download your PDF receipt or receive SMS confirmation.
💡 You can also SMS your plate number to 2220 from any UAE mobile line to get an instant fine summary — no internet needed.
