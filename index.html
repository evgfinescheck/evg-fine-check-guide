Traffic Fines

What is EVG Fine Check?

Emirates Vehicle Gate (EVG) is the UAE's official platform that consolidates traffic fines from all seven emirates in one place.

The check EVG traffic fines service allows UAE vehicle owners to instantly view outstanding fines using their plate number, Emirates ID, or Traffic Code Number.

⚠️ Unpaid EVG fines attract a 50% late penalty automatically.
